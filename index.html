How it's made
The Naula method.
A discipline, not a brand name.
01 · Sourcing Named botanicals. Single origin.

Whole plants from Long An, Vietnam. Every ingredient named. Nothing blended or anonymous.

02 · Naula preparation Slow-cooked whole.

Time, fire, and water — adjusted for each ingredient. No universal formula applied to everything.
