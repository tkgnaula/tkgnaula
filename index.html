The product
What is TKG Naula botanical powder — and how is it different from regular tea?
Regular tea is brewed by steeping dried leaves and discarding the solids — you only receive what dissolves in water. TKG Naula botanical powder is prepared through the Naula method: the whole plant is processed in full, then freeze-dried at sub-zero temperatures to preserve its natural properties, colour, and character. When you prepare it, you use everything — nothing is discarded.

Dual-chamber design: botanical powder and fresh honey are sealed separately. The honey is not pre-mixed because sweetness is your choice — and fresh honey requires proper preservation. No refined sugar. No preservatives.
What are the wellness benefits of Lư Trung Hoả and Phú Đăng Hoả?
Both products belong to the Hoả (Fire) line — designed to support internal warmth and thermal balance, not to treat or cure. The Hoả line retains its warming character whether prepared hot or cold.

Ginger & lemongrass (Lư Trung Hoả): warming, circulatory support, resilience. Perilla & clove (Phú Đăng Hoả): calming, anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, internal balance.

Both tested by Eurofins ISO/IEC 17025 — no detectable lead, no microbial contamination.
What is the difference in taste between the two?
Lư Trung Hoả (Ginger & Lemongrass): bold and direct. Spicy heat combined with bright, fresh aroma creates immediate alertness. Warmth arrives quickly and clearly — suited for when you need energy to begin.

Phú Đăng Hoả (Perilla & Clove): quiet and layered. Perilla carries a gentle, slightly cooling herbal note; clove adds a deep, slow warmth underneath. Warmth arrives gradually — suited for winding down and finding stillness.
Why does the powder colour vary between batches?
Slight colour variation between batches is a sign of real ingredients — not a production error. Colour depends on harvest season and growing conditions. TKG Naula does not standardise colour through additives — standardisation happens through the Naula method and independent testing.
