Three principles, applied consistently across every formulation. Not trend-driven. Not export for export's sake. The same discipline in the kitchen and in the cup — whole leaves and roots, named at origin, prepared through the Naula method.

01 Source
Sourced
Whole botanicals.
Named at origin.

Real plants — Ginger, Lemongrass, Lime, Perilla, Clove, Kumquat — sourced whole and named on the label. No flavouring, no anonymous "natural extract." Every ingredient traces back to a species, a place, a method.

02 Naula
Prepare
Naula.
Freeze-dried.

Whole leaves and roots simmered slowly in water: not extracted under pressure, not isolated into concentrate. Just heat, water, and time. Freeze-drying preserves volatile aromatics and colour without heat damage. No concentrates, no chemical stabilisers. The plant arrives in your cup the way it left the field.

03 Present
Present
Honest formulation.
Nothing added.

No preservatives. No artificial colour. No artificial sweeteners. Clean formulation, laboratory tested by Quatest 3. The product is allowed to taste like itself.
