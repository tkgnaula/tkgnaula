Why a seed

We could have given you a single-use gift, but we chose to hand you a seed. Because things of true value rarely appear overnight. A seed needs time to sprout, a habit needs time to form, and a healthy body needs time to be nurtured. We call this approach Naula — not seeking shortcuts, but embracing steady preparation, day by day.
