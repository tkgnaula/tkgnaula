The story behind TKG
Our position

Not trend-driven.
Not export
for export's sake.

TKG started from a single question: why does a product rooted in Vietnamese herbal tradition have to strip out everything that makes it Vietnamese to be sellable?

The Naula method is not a marketing device. It is a preparation discipline — one that existed before the brand did. The name was given to make the practice legible, not to make it sound exotic.

"The same discipline in the kitchen and in the cup — whole leaves and roots, named at origin."

What we sell is the result of that discipline applied consistently. No extracts. No anonymised inputs. No flavouring added to compensate for what was removed.

The discipline

Three principles.
Applied without exception.

Applied consistently across every formulation — not as aspiration, but as constraint.

01
Source
Whole botanicals.
Named at origin.

Every ingredient traces back to a species, a place, a method. Real plants — sourced whole from Vietnamese cooperatives in Long An. Named on the label. No flavouring. No anonymous "natural extract" that could mean anything.

02
Naula · Prepare
Adjusted for
each ingredient.

Fresh botanicals are prepared into liquid form through the Naula method — where heat, water, and time are calibrated individually for each plant. The liquid is then frozen and freeze-dried under vacuum. Not the same process for every ingredient. Each one, separately.

03
Present
Honest formulation.
Nothing added.

No preservatives. No artificial colour. No artificial sweeteners. The product is allowed to taste like itself — including the sediment from ginger starch, including the colour shift from perilla anthocyanin. These are not flaws to be corrected.

Naula preparation · Long An, Vietnam

Ingredients

Six botanicals.
Every one named.

TKG uses six botanicals across two formulations. All are sourced whole. All are named by species on the label. There are no proprietary blends, no undisclosed inputs.

The sourcing is direct: purchased from cooperatives in Long An province, Vietnam. Traceability is not a feature. It is the default.
